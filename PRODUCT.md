# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Indian medical students and interns preparing for highly competitive PG examinations (NEET PG, FMGE, INI-CET). They are operating under immense stress, severe sleep deprivation, and tight schedules, often practicing in short 5-minute bursts during ward rounds, subway commutes, or late-night library sessions.

## Product Purpose
To provide a 100% free, high-yield, offline-first MCQ prep platform that eliminates financial friction and visual clutter. Success is defined by active daily engagement, effortless offline practice in hospital basements, and high retention rates, showing that top-tier prep does not require a ₹25,000 subscription.

## Brand Personality
*   **3-Word Personality**: Handcrafted, Calming, Collaborative.
*   **Voice & Tone**: High-credibility, peer-to-peer solidarity, completely honest. No sales speak, no synthetic AI jargon.
*   **Emotional Goals**: Relief from subscription anxiety, visual calm amidst study stress, and the excitement of joining a community movement.

## Anti-references
*   **Commercial Prep Apps**: Stuffy, database-heavy, cluttered with banner ads, upselling notices, and locked icons.
*   **Generic AI Slop**: Dark-mode grids with purple/neon glows, floating cards, text gradients, and lazy "icon + title + text" repeating columns.
*   **Copywriting Triggers**: Words like "Revolutionize", "Seamless", "Harness the power of AI", "Next-gen", or "Empower".

## Design Principles
1.  **Zero Barriers to Entry**: Allow practice immediately (Guest Mode) with zero onboarding friction. 
2.  **Calm & Uncluttered Canvas**: Use warm neutrals and soft space to offset the density of medical syllabus content.
3.  **Active Recall Loops**: Leverage the Zeigarnik effect to make unfinished progress feel like a loop that needs closing.
4.  **Clinical Credibility**: Maintain rigorous layout precision and peer-reviewed clarity to convey medical authority.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
*   High-contrast elements for tired, strained eyes studying in low-light environments.
*   Simple keyboard and tap targets (minimum 44x44px) to ease interaction for busy interns on the move.
*   Optimized for low-bandwidth cellular environments (local caching via IndexedDB).
